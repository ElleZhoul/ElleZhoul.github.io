Ambiance

Thoughts drift on morning air
Blue sky peeks through trees
The sun begins to warm the ground
Verdant life stirs on the edge of a blade

Startled animals scatter
A gentle wind blows on
The chorus of life
I lean back and hear

Faint, familiar
It calls from memory
It calls from memory
Forgotten, treasured
An inaudible noise

I sense a presence nearby
In the calm and well-being
Something amiss
I know its name
It means no harm

This is my last farewell
To these, the memories of
A simpler time
In this small corner of the world
Where time stands still
